Hi [NAME],

Workshop 4 is coming up on [DATE] and I wanted to get you the pre-read
ahead of time.

This session is a little different to the ones before it. We have spent
three workshops building a clear picture of what the platform costs. Today
we look at the other side of the equation — what the platform actually
enables.

The goal is to build a value and risk scorecard that sits alongside the
cost model. Without it, there is a real risk that cost becomes the only
lens leadership uses to evaluate the platform — and that can lead to
decisions that reduce spend but increase risk, slow delivery, or damage
developer experience.

The scorecard uses DORA and SPACE as reference frameworks. These are
industry-standard approaches to measuring software delivery performance
and developer productivity. You do not need to know them in detail —
the pre-read covers what you need.

Before the session it would be helpful to have a rough sense of:

- Your current deployment frequency and lead time — even a rough estimate
- Any incident data from the last quarter — volume and recovery times
- Any existing developer satisfaction or productivity signals, even informal

Nothing needs to be formal. Rough is fine. We will agree proxies in the
session where data is not available.

If anything raises questions before we meet, feel free to get in touch.

See you on [DATE] at [TIME].
